FAQ

  • Who created SketchKeys?

    My name is Artiom and I’m a Product Designer at WeWork. My parents (both graphic designers) are helping me with the production and shipping process. Feel free to drop me a line.

  • How long the shipping takes?

    Shipping around Europe is taking about 8-15 days, US – 11-22 days, Asia and Oceania – 14-30 days.

  • Is peeling off the stickers going to damage my keyboard?

    Absolutely not! While working on the product I made at least a dozen of cycles of putting stickers and peeling them off. Both my MacBook and external keyboards are in a great shape!

  • What if I don’t like the product?

    We work hard to make sure it won’t happen. If you don’t like the product you can get a refund 30 days since the shipping date.

  • How durable are the stickers?

    Stickers are made to last at least for a year, giving you enough time to remember all the shortcuts combinations 💪

  • I’m colorblind and I want to use your product.

    Send me an email and I’ll create a custom version for you for the same price.

  • What if Sketch creators are going to change the shortcuts?

    I’ve got an informal confirmation from Bohemian Coding that there are no plans to change the shortcuts functionality in the near future.

  • I like the stickers but I’d love to get it for other software besides Sketch and Xcode.

    Sounds interesting! Send me an email and I’ll see what I can do.
