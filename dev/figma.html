FAQ

  • Who created SketchKeys?

    SketchKeys is a family-run project, managed by Artiom. I'm a former Senior Product Designer at WeWork and author of "Solving Product Design Exercises". My parents (both product designers) are taking care of the production and shipping process. Feel free to reach out.

  • How long the shipping takes?

    Shipping around Europe is taking about 8-15 days, US – 11-22 days, Asia and Oceania – 14-30 days.

  • Is peeling off the stickers going to damage my keyboard?

    No, while working on the product I made at least a dozen of cycles of putting stickers and peeling them off. Both my MacBook and external keyboards are in a great shape!

  • What if I don’t like the product?

    We work hard to make sure it won’t happen. If you don’t like the product you can get a refund 40 days since the shipping date.

  • How durable are the stickers?

    Stickers are made to last at least for a year, giving you enough time to remember all the shortcuts combinations 💪
